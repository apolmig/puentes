                                        Who it helps

                                        Built for people translating civic noise into something usable fast.

                                        The product is tuned for teams who need enough AI to move faster, but not so much that the handoff stops being accountable.

                                        Educators

                                        Reset the room fast

                                        Move from rumor to discussion prompt or explainer without scrambling across tabs and screenshots.

                                        Youth orgs

                                        Respond before rumor sticks

                                        Turn a packet and community question into a reviewed answer before the wrong framing hardens.

                                        Trusted creators

                                        Publish without feeding the noise

                                        Keep the hook usable, the sourcing visible, and the final call human.

                                        Editors

                                        Audit the AI path

                                        Track which model ran what, what it suggested, and why the final export was approved.

                                        Trust layer

                                        The trust model is visible in the interface, not hidden in policy copy.

                                        Packet-first generation

                                        The app keeps packet facts, AI interpretation, and export-ready copy visibly separate.

                                        Review copilot, not autopilot

                                        AI can flag citation drift, certainty inflation, and manipulation risk, but approval stays human.

                                        Model desk

                                        Teams can choose among approved models per workflow without exposing API keys in the browser.

                                        Read-only sharing

                                        Share links present the package without opening the editing flow, so context can travel without accidental changes.
