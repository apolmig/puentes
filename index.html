                          Why this product exists

                          Make civic media easier to use, not easier to weaponize.

                          Puentes is not trying to tell young people what to think. It is trying to make it easier for trusted messengers to explain what is documented, what is overstated, and what is still unresolved.

                          Creator-native

                          Made for the handoff

                          Trusted creators should leave with something they can post, adapt, or remix, not a page of abstract guidance.

                          Source visible

                          Receipts stay attached

                          Citations, uncertainty, and missing context stay visible so the output can travel without faking certainty.

                          Youth aware

                          Current, not cringe

                          The voice aims for sharp, familiar, practical language rather than policy PDF tone or engagement bait.

                          Viral on purpose

                          Easy to share responsibly

                          Read-only preview links and remix paths help good civic explainers move faster than rumor loops.

                          Trust layer

                          Built to reduce manipulation, not just produce content faster.

                          Manipulation detection

                          Flags fear hooks, false binaries, scapegoating, and outrage packaging designed to trigger identity-first reactions.

                          Amplification awareness

                          Shows when a claim is spreading because it is algorithmically loud, not because it is well sourced.

                          Human review gate

                          Every export is handoff-ready, not publish-ready. Real people still decide what should go live.

                          Read-only sharing

                          Share links present the package without opening the editing flow, so context can travel without accidental changes.

                          Blueprint

                          The system underneath the creator flow.

                          01

                          Source packet

                          Documents, reporting, and community questions stay grouped with provenance, date, and trust context.

                          02

                          Claim check

                          Claims are separated into supported, mixed, and unclear so the draft starts from what the packet can actually defend.

                          03

                          Format engine

                          The same packet can become a caption, a short script, a carousel pack, or a classroom prompt without losing the source trail.

                          04

                          Human gate

                          Checklist state, notes, approvals, and revisions live per packet so the handoff does not reset every time someone switches topics.

                          05

                          Export loop

                          Approved packets create copyable outputs, downloadable handoffs, and share previews designed for responsible distribution.

                          06

                          Open trust model

                          The experience remains auditable through visible sources, documented constraints, and explicit non-autoposting behavior.
