                            Who it's for

                            Built for people who need to catch the lie and publish the correction fast.

                            Puentes is for the people translating civic noise into something grounded, usable, and safe to pass on inside the feed.

                            Educators

                            Reset the room fast

                            Move from rumor to discussion prompt or explainer without scrambling across tabs, screenshots, and reaction clips.

                            Youth orgs

                            Respond before rumor sticks

                            Turn a packet and community question into a reviewed answer before the wrong framing hardens into the only version people see.

                            Trusted creators

                            Publish without feeding the noise

                            Keep the hook usable, the sourcing visible, and the final call human, even when the feed wants the sharpest outrage.

                            Responsible sharing

                            Help context travel

                            Read-only previews and export packages let good civic explainers move farther without losing receipts.

                            Detector layer

                            Trust is visible here, not decorative.

                            Manipulation detection

                            Flags fear hooks, false binaries, scapegoating, and outrage packaging designed to trigger identity-first reactions.

                            Amplification awareness

                            Shows when a claim is spreading because it is algorithmically loud, not because it is well sourced or actually new.

                            Human review gate

                            Every export is handoff-ready, not publish-ready. Real people still decide what should go live.

                            Read-only sharing

                            Share links present the package without opening the editing flow, so context can travel without accidental changes.

                            System

                            The structure that keeps the handoff credible.

                            01

                            Source packet

                            Documents, reporting, and community questions stay grouped with provenance, date, and trust context.

                            02

                            Claim check

                            Claims are separated into supported, mixed, and unclear so the draft starts from what the packet can actually defend.

                            03

                            Format engine

                            The same packet can become a caption, a short script, a carousel pack, or a classroom prompt without losing the source trail.

                            04

                            Human gate

                            Checklist state, notes, approvals, and revisions live per packet so the handoff does not reset every time someone switches topics.

                            05

                            Export loop

                            Approved packets create copyable outputs, downloadable handoffs, and share previews designed for responsible distribution.

                            06

                            Open trust model

                            The experience remains auditable through visible sources, documented constraints, and explicit non-autoposting behavior.
